What About Tracey

I believe that a Ui / Ux designer has to be able to design and redesign portals from the ground up.

I have over 18 years of experience in building high quality software using the principles and techniques of Ui/Ux design and development. I am adept at finding user needs, building empathy, articulating ideas through stories and visual design, task flows and successfully delivering beautifully clean designs.

My history is of effectively communicating and cross-functional team leadership with Ui & Ux team members, other visual designers, product owners, developers, executive leadership team and stakeholders. I especially enjoy working with developers and engineers as I began as one. I am extremely flexible, able to think on my feet, creative and pragmatic, with strong people, written, visual and verbal skills. Flexible enough to tactfully lobby stakeholders, POs and developers for the best solution. I believe that your best success comes from your worst failure.

...and I'm an awesome cook!
